Serenity

 

Welcome on arrival at Colombo International Airport by Voyages Ceylan representative, transfer to Colombo. Check-in at Hotel.

On the second day, after breakfast Check-out from Hotel & proceed to Sigiriya, En route visit Elephant Orphanage at Pinnawela. There you can see the bottle feeding of baby Elephants. See them at play. Accompany them to a river close by to see them having their daily bath.

Continue to Sigiriya. Evening climb the 5th century A.D Rock Fortress. See the mural paintings of “Heavenly Maidens” over 1500 years old, which are in a sheltered pocket of the rock. Historians say originally there were close to 500 paintings but today only 19 exist. Also visit the water and rock gardens and the King’s bath on the 600 feet palace fortress. You would also see the famous Sigiriya Graffiti which dates back to the 6th Century.

Overnight at “Sigiriya Village”

On the third day, after breakfast proceed to Kandy. En route visit a Batik Factory and a Spice Garden at Matale. Here you would be shown the different varieties of spices grown and demonstration of their use.

Continue to Kandy. Tour the City of Kandy including visits to the Art and Crafts Centre, Gem Museum and Lapidary and the world famous Temple of The Sacred Tooth Relic Of Lord Buddha. Evening view a “ Kandyan Folkloric Dance “ performance. ( Cultural Show ).

Dinner & overnight stay at “Hotel Thilanka”

On The fourth day, after breakfast visit Century old Botanical Garden at Peradeniya, with its amazing verities of trees, plants and flowers. Reputed to be one of the best in the world with its hundreds of verities of Roses and Orchids.

Drive back to Kandy.

Dinner & overnight stay at “Hotel Thilanka”

On the fifth day after breakfast proceed to Nuwara Eliya. Nuwara-Eliya – The “Little England” of Sri Lanka. The journey will take you through some of Sri Lanka’s beautiful scenic locations. Enjoy the drive through green carpeted areas of tea plantations. Nuwara-Eliya nesting below Pidurutalagala, Sri Lanka’s highest mountain is set against beautiful backdrops of Mountains, Valleys, Waterfalls & Tea Plantations – offers beauty unlimited.

Sightseeing tour of Nuwara-Eliya. During site seeing you can visit several Fruit & Vegetable Farms around Nuwara-Eliya. Whatever they grow in the misty hills of Sri Lanka, you can make that they are beautifully done and superbly maintained.

Dinner & overnight stay at “Alpine Hotel”

On the sixth day, after breakfast Check-out from Hotel & proceed to Tissamaharama. En route visit Ella. Ella is a beautiful small village in Sri Lanka's hill country with little more than a handful of shops, hotels and guesthouses, but it has an almost perfect climate and occupies a very scenic vantage point, with views on a fine day stretching right across the South Coast of Sri Lanka. Continue to Tissamaharama

Evening visit Kataragama. Kataragama is a pilgrimage town popular with Buddhist, Hindu,Muslim and indigenous Vedda communities of Sri Lanka and South India. The town has Ruhunu Maha Kataragama devalaya, a shrine dedicated to Skanda-Murukan also known as Kataragamadevio.

Dinner & overnight stay at “Hotel Priyankara”

On the seventh day, after breakfast check-out from Hotel & proceed to Galle. Visit “The Southern Fotress City” Galle. Visit its oldest Landmark, the massive Portuguese and Dutch Fort in which the central city is contained.

After proceed to Beruwala

Dinner & overnight stay at “The Palms”

On the eighth day, after Breakfast Excursion to Bentota (Optional). Bentota is a tourist attraction, with a local airport (Bentota River Airport) and a handful of world-class hotels. It is a destination for water sports. It is the hosting land for the famous Sri Lankan Jeweler Aida. Bentota also delivers an ancient art of healing called Ayurveda. Bentota is famous for its toddy production, an alcoholic beverage made out of coconut nectar.

Dinner & overnight stay at “The Palms”

After Breakfast spend the at leisure on the ninth day

Dinner & overnight stay at “The Palms”

On the tenth day, after Breakfast Check-out from hotel & proceed to the Airport to take the connecting flight on time.               

End of Tour.......
