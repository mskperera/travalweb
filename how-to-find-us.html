How To Find Us

ROAD DIRECTIONS TO HIDDEN HILLS ESTATE

  1. Travel on the Colombo/Awissawella main road (A4).
  2. After the 53rd Kilometer post you will come across a Railway crossing at the Puwakpitiya Railway station. 
  3. Soon after you will see the Puwakpitiya Junction and take the Thunmodara Road turn off to your right at the Puwakpitiya Junction.
  4. (2 Land Marks : Buddha Statue at the turn off and a bridge soon after)
  5. Travel 4.7 KM’s on  the Puwakpitiya/Thunmodara road to Digana Junction.
  6. At 4.7.KM’s on your left you will see Gete Mawara Mawatha, Rabutan Plantation board  and the HIDDEN HILLS ESTATE signage, take the left turn and keep to left.
  7. After 0.5 KM’s on the Gete Mawara Mawatha, you will see a HIDDEN HILLS ESTATE signage on your left. Take the left Concrete Road between the Rubber Estate. Travel 0.3 KM’s to the Black gate soon after the sharp right turn.
  8. Follow the HIDDEN HILLS ESTATE signage and once you enter the estate follow the Estate road and climb right up to the car park.
