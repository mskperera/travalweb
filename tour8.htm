HAVE FUN TOUR

 

Day – 1

 

   

 

Welcome on arrival at Colombo International Airport by Voyages Ceylan representative, transfer to Kandy. En route visit Elephant Orphanage at Pinnawela. There you can see the bottle feeding of baby Elephants. See them at play. Accompany them to a river close by to see them having their daily bath.

 

Continue to Kandy. Tour the City of Kandy including visits to the Art and Crafts Centre, Gem Museum and Lapidary and the world famous Temple of The Sacred Tooth Relic Of Lord Buddha. Evening view a “ Kandyan Folkloric Dance “ performance. (Cultural Show).

Dinner & Overnight stay at “Serene Garden Hotel”

 

Day – 2

  

 

 

After breakfast visit Century old Botanical Garden at Peradeniya, with its amazing verities of trees, plants and flowers. Reputed to be one of the best in the world with its hundreds of verities of Roses and Orchids.

 

After proceed to Beruwala. En route visit Colombo. Visit the island’s metropolitan capital and its places of interest such as Independent Squire, Fort, Pettah and Residential Area and the Shopping Tour.

Dinner & Overnight stay at “The Palms”


Day – 3

 

 

On the third day, after breakfast spend the day at leisure.

Dinner & Overnight stay at “The Palms”

 

Day – 4

 

    

 

After Breakfast check-out from hotel & proceed to the Airport to take the connecting flight on time.
