Directions

  • Travel on the Colombo/Awissawella main road (A4) from Colombo to Awissawella.
  • After the 53rd Kilometer post you will come across a Railway crossing at the Puwakpitiya Railway station.
  • Soon after you will see the Puwakpitiya Junction and take the Thunmodara Road turn off to your right at the Puwakpitiya Junction.
  • (2 Land Marks : Buddha Statue at the turn off and a bridge soon after)
  • Travel 4.7 KM’s on the Puwakpitiya/Thunmodara road to Digana Junction.
  • At 4.7.KM’s on your left you will see Gete Mawara Mawatha, Rambutan Plantation board and the HIDDEN HILLS ESTATE (HHEPL) signage, take the left turn and keep to left.
  • After 0.5 KM’s on Gete Mawara Mawatha, you will see a HIDDEN HILLS ESTATE (HHEPL) signage on your left. Take the left Concrete Road between the Rubber Estates. Travel 0.3 KM’s to the Black gate soon after the sharp right turn. Climb up another 0.7 KM’s to the car park.
