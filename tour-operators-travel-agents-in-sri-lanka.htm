TOUR OPERATORS IN SRI LANKA


All travel agents and tour operators in Sri Lanka are capable of offering cost effective and safe tours that includes large doses of friendliness. Tour operators in Sri Lanka with decades of experience in the travel industry could be found as a part of large group companies who also hold IATA accreditation. There are many lesser known tour operators in Sri Lanka in every city who are equally reputed and are good at offering intriguing tour packages with popular sightseeing tours, special itineraries, tour packages for small groups or even individual travelers. Almost all tour operators in Sri Lanka offer tour packages that include ticketing, transportation, reserving accommodation whether the choice is a 3 star, 5 star, boutique style or simple basic accommodation and guided tours. Interpreter services are also available to ensure better value packages. Those wishing to couple romance in the tour would be glad to know that it’s not uncommon for visitors to request the tour operator to arrange a wedding in a traditional Sri Lankan setting.

 

TRAVEL AGENTS IN SRI LANKA


The Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority has a listing of practicing travel agents registered with them who offer a comprehensive service to visitors to Sri Lanka. Travel Agents in Sri Lanka need to go through a comprehensive procedure to obtain approval from the Authority and are considered not only competent, but safe. The Travel Agents Association of Sri Lanka, a national body that represents the interests of a large complement of Travel Agents in Sri Lanka has been around from the early 70’s. Backed by such assurances and the diverse variety of attractions that compact little Sri Lanka could offer, whether on land or in the seas surrounding it, travelling has become a pastime with locals as well as tourists. Today travel agents vie with one another to provide unique travel packages at highly competitive rates. Large hotel chains have their own travel arm that offer tour packages that conveniently include their chain hotels in key tourist destinations around the island. For those who are tired of boutique and luxury hotels there are a great many simple hotels that provides clean A/c rooms, good bathrooms and internet services at a decent price.
