a few words about us

 

Establishing our own identify in 1994, we are one of the leading inbound tour operators in Sri Lanka. York Tours is a member of the Sri Lanka Association of Inbound Tour Operators (SLAITO) & the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority (SLTDA).Based upon our nerve center in Colombo we operate in every tourist location in the island backed by our group hotels namely, “The Palms” – Beruwala, “Sigiriya Village” – Sigiriya, “Club Palm Bay” – Marawila & “Fisherman’s Bay – Weligama. We also work with many other hotels in Sri Lanka with extensions to the Maldives to give variety, satisfaction & purpose.

We offer itineraries that not only meet with your leisure requirement but also give purpose to your tour by facilitating it as per your budget.

Experience moments of charm and warmth of Asia in Sri Lanka, The Pearl of the Indian Ocean for a breathtaking holiday that will live in your hearts for years to come.

Come & join with us as we assure an unforgettable holiday in this paradise island.
